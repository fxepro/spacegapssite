Do’s

  • Keep God’s Word close to your heart

  • Pursue mercy and truth

  • Trust in the Lord with all your heart

  • Acknowledge Him in all your ways

  • Fear the Lord

  • Honor the Lord with your possessions

  • Honor the Lord with your first fruits

  • Seek wisdom

  • Seek understanding

  • Keep sound wisdom and discretion

  • Walk humbly and receive God’s grace

  • Be wise and inherit His glory

Don’ts

  • Do not be wise in your own eyes

  • Do not do evil

  • Do not despise the chastening of the Lord

  • Do not fear sudden terror

  • Do not withhold what is due to anyone

  • Do not delay doing good when it is in your power to act

  • Do not devise evil against your neighbor

  • Stay away from oppressors; never become one

  • Do not envy your prospering neighbor

  • Do not fight without cause

  • Do not be wicked

  • Do not be a fool
